Refund and Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: March 15, 2026

1. Cancellations

Orders can only be canceled if they have not yet been dispatched for delivery. For in-store digital payments, cancellation is not possible once the gate exit QR code has been generated. To request a cancellation, you must contact support@twinmart.com immediately with your Order ID.

2. Refunds for Failed Payments

If your money was deducted but the application shows the payment as "Failed", the amount will automatically be refunded to your original payment method by our gateway partner (Razorpay) within 5 to 7 working days.

3. Return & Refund Eligibility

If you receive a damaged or incorrect product, you may request a refund within 3 days of receiving the item. Please email detailed photos of the product and your receipt to support@twinmart.com. Approved refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 7 working days.

4. Non-Refundable Items

Perishable goods, personalized items, and digital assets are strictly non-refundable once purchased.
